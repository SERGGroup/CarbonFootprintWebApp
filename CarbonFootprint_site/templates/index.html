Info
  • Insert the number of meals that you prepare in a typical day. Counting only 1 time meals done together with family members. The total impact of meals is divided by the number of family members

Info
  • Insert the number of times that your family use the oven. The impact of the oven is not considered into the 'Food cooking' part.The total impact of oven utilization is divided by the number of family members
